About Tiffany's Pool Tables Kenya

Serving Kenyan Businesses Since 2012

We specialise in supplying reliable, good‑looking pool tables to Kenyan bars, clubs, lounges and estates. Every table is built to handle busy nights and everyday use.

From town centres like Nairobi CBD and Thika Road to neighbourhood spots in Rongai and Mombasa, our teams deliver, install and level your table so it is ready to earn from day one.

Whether you run a small kibanda, a modern sports bar or a student hostel, we help you choose a table that fits your budget, space and target customers.
