About ElitePool

Crafting Excellence Since 1995

At ElitePool, we combine decades of craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to create the finest pool tables in the world. Each table is a masterpiece of design and engineering.

Our artisans use only premium materials: solid hardwoods, Italian slate, and championship-grade felt. Whether for professional tournaments or home entertainment, our tables deliver exceptional playability and timeless elegance.

With over 25 years of experience, we've supplied tables to championship venues, luxury hotels, and discerning customers worldwide.
